Waffles

Description

Same ingredients as pancakes, but different ratios. Waffles are crispy on the outside, soft and fluffy on the inside. The textural contrast makes this dish a loveable one for the breakfast spread.

Ingredients

Waffle Batter

  • 2 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 1/2 tbsp caster sugar
  • Pinch of cooking/kosher salt
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla essence
  • 4 large eggs
  • 150g unsalted butter

Topping ideas

  • Butter
  • Maple syrup
  • Icing sugar
  • Strawberries, blueberries or other fruit
  • Whipped cream
  • Jams, marmalades, compotes
  • Ice cream

Steps

  1. Whisk dry: Place the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in a large mixing bowl. Whisk to combine.
  2. Whisk in wet: Add milk, eggs, and vanilla. Whisk to combine. Add melted butter then whisk until lump free. The batter should be pourable but thick - slightly thinner than pancakes.
  3. Recommended resting: Cover then refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight. This will create softer insides and better flavour. If you have no time to wait, proceed immediately instead to cooking.
  4. Preheat a waffle maker.
  5. Cooking: Use a ladle to pour batter in to just cover the iron. Don't get greedy or it will leak out the sides. Cook until golden and crisp (3-6 minutes depending on your waffle maker).
  6. Serving: Transfer onto plates and serve as you go, with any toppings you like. If you are not eating immediately, put on a rack and keep warm in a preheated oven (70C) while you continue cooking.
