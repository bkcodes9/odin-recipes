Pepperoni Pizza

Description

This pepperoni pizza recipe is quick, easy and tasty! Delicious homemade pizzas have never been easier.

Ingredients

Pizza Sauce

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 12 ounces tomata pasta
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Pizza Crust

  • 3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 ounce yeast
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/3 cups warm water
  • 1/3 cup neutral oil

Toppings

  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 6 ounce pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C. Grease two 12-inch pizza pans
  2. Make Sauce: Whisk together all sauce ingredients in a medium bowl until smooth and combined. Set aside
  3. Make Crust: Combine 2 cups flour, yeast, sugar and salt in a large bowl. Add warm water and oil; mix until well blended, about 1 minute. Gradually add remaining flour, a little at a time, until a soft, sticky dough forms
  4. Transfer dough to a floured surface; knead until dough is smooth and elastic, about 4 minutes. Add more flour as needed. Let dough rest for 10 minutes
  5. Divide dough in half. Lightly flour your hands, then pat each piece of dough onto the prepared pizza pans.
  6. Top dough with sauce, cheese and pepperoni
  7. Bake pizzas in oven until crusts are browned and cheese is bubbly, about 18-20 minutes. Rotate the pizza pans between the top and bottom oven racks halfway through baking.
  8. Serve immediately
